# A Tribute from Gary Boycan
### Former husband and lifelong friend

---

Nancy and I met in 1959. For some magical reason the W and B were sat together at one of the work tables in 11th grade physics class. I had always been drawn to the prettiest, smartest girl in my classes and heaven actually ended up sitting next to me. Not only sitting beside each other we had to work together as a team on projects during the semester. One thing led to another and by that January when I got my driver’s license we were already going steady. The love we had found continued through undergraduate and graduate schools and we finally married in 1965. We first lived in Detroit and then moved to Louisville where our sons, Steven and David were born and then onto Reston, Va.

Our marriage ended in 1976 but our love and friendship never died. We continued to work together to raise our sons, to enjoy several successful real estate partnerships, and socialize on family occasions over the years. I visited her last week on her death bed and kissed my first love goodbye. She was a wonderful presence in my life and I will always cherish her memory.

— Gary Boycan
